### members(name) 

Get a clans member list with exp gained and total kills

**Parameters**

**name**: , The clans name

**Returns**: `Object`, Member list

**Example**:
```js
api.rs.hiscores.clan('Efficiency Experts').then(function(clan) {  console.log(clan);}).catch(console.error);
```
